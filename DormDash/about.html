About

DormDash is a dedicated campus-only platform designed to make student life easier and more connected. Whether you need to borrow a charger, get last-minute notes, find affordable second-hand items, or offer help to a peer, DormDash brings it all together in one place. Students living in hostels, dorms, or apartments can post requests or offers, respond in real time, and even earn points for lending a hand.
